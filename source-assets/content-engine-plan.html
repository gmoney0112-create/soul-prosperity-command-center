1. Operating rules

  1. Every post has one destination: freebie opt-in, $7 ebook, or Skool.
  2. Don't pitch every day. The ratio is roughly 2 value + 1 story + 1 pitch per 4 posts.
  3. One source pillar per week. Repurpose the same pillar across formats.
  4. Hooks live or die in the first 1.5 seconds. Open with motion + a sentence that creates a question.
  5. Captions exist to extend dwell, not to repeat the video. Three lines max above the fold.
  6. Single CTA per post. Bio link routes to socialBioLink in config.js.

6. Repurposing pipeline

One pillar per week. The pillar is a long-form thought (a 12-minute YouTube video, a podcast episode, or a deep journal entry). From that pillar, the week's outputs:

  1. 2 short-form video Reels/TikToks — 15–45s clips with one idea each.
  2. 1 carousel — repackaged as 5–7 slides on Instagram + LinkedIn.
  3. 1 quote graphic — the strongest line, designed.
  4. 1 long caption / X thread — 5–7 tweets connected.
  5. 1 email broadcast — same idea, different voice, link to $7 ebook.
  6. 1 SMS broadcast — single-line tease + bio link, only on pitch days.
Cadence guard. If a week is missed, do not double-post the next week. Catch up by reusing evergreen Reels. The engine is built to survive bad weeks; do not punish your audience for them.
