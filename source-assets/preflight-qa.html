Launch Blueprint · Preflight QA

The checklist before traffic flips on.

A human-runnable preflight that a non-technical operator can walk top to bottom in 60 minutes. Every item is a yes/no test against a real test contact and a real test transaction. If any item fails, ads do not turn on.

Rule. Use a real throwaway email and a real test card. The point is to flush out problems no validator can catch — broken links, missing footers, wrong product images, refund flow that doesn't fire.

Section A · Static dashboard

Configuration & UI

  • config.js has no placeholders — open the GHL Wiring panel; "required missing" is 0 and "placeholders" is 0 for production fields.
  • npm run check passes — node --check + validator + serverless smoke all green.
  • Dashboard renders on prod URL — open the live Vercel URL on desktop and mobile; readiness meter renders, links resolve.
  • Theme toggle works — light/dark switches without breaking layout.
  • Asset dock links open — every asset-dock a in the launch panel resolves to a non-404 page.

Section B · Funnel pages

Sales pages, opt-ins, checkouts

  • $7 sales page loads — buy button uses real Stripe-connected GHL checkout link; price displays as $7.
  • Order bump checkbox — $17 audio appears on the $7 checkout, fires purchase_audio17 when ticked.
  • Upsell page → $27 paperback — accept and decline both route somewhere sensible.
  • Course $67 sales page — direct purchase fires purchase_course67 and applies buyer-course67.
  • Skool trial link — skoolTrial resolves to the live Skool group join URL.
  • Freebie opt-in — submitting creates a contact, applies lead-freebie, captures UTMs into custom fields.
  • Privacy policy + terms linked — every checkout, footer, and opt-in page links to current legal docs.

Section C · GHL automation

Workflows fire end-to-end

  • WF-01 freebie nurture — opt-in test contact receives Day 0 delivery email within 5 minutes.
  • WF-02 $7 buyer delivery — real $7 test transaction triggers PDF delivery email and applies buyer-ebook7.
  • WF-03 audio recovery — buyer without buyer-audio17 receives bridge email at H+2.
  • WF-04 paperback upgrade — Day 2 pitch email lands in inbox.
  • WF-05 course pitch — Day 5 method teaser arrives.
  • WF-06 Skool trial invite — course buyer receives invite within 30 minutes.
  • WF-07 annual / lifetime — monthly subscriber at day 21 receives annual pitch (use a date-shifted test contact to verify).
  • WF-08 cart save — abandoned checkout for 30+ minutes triggers abandoned-checkout tag and save sequence.
  • Tag-based exit conditions — apply buyer-course67 mid-WF-03; verify the contact exits.

Section D · Tracking

Pixel, UTMs, attribution

  • Meta Pixel Helper green — opens on sales page with view_content firing.
  • begin_checkout fires — visible in pixel helper and in Events Manager test mode.
  • purchase_ebook7 fires once — refresh thank-you page; should NOT double-fire.
  • UTM passthrough — click a utm_source=meta&utm_term=authority test link, complete opt-in; GHL contact has utm_source=meta, utm_term=authority in custom fields.
  • Server-side conversions (if configured) — Conversions API event lands in Events Manager within 5 minutes of test purchase.
  • Refund event flows through — issue test refund; refunded tag applied, contact suppressed from retargeting audiences.

Section E · Compliance & deliverability

Legal & inbox

  • Email footer — physical address + unsubscribe link present on every WF email.
  • Unsubscribe works — click in test email, contact tagged unsubscribed-email, no further sends.
  • SMS STOP works — reply STOP, contact tagged sms-opt-out, no further SMS.
  • Quiet hours — no SMS scheduled between 9pm and 9am contact-local.
  • SPF / DKIM / DMARC — sending domain authenticated; mail-tester or similar shows 9+/10.
  • Inbox placement — test deliveries land in primary, not promotions, on Gmail / Apple Mail / Outlook.

Section F · Operations

Support, refunds, monitoring

  • Support inbox — replies from contact land somewhere a human will see them within 24h.
  • Refund SLA — refund processed inside 48h, refund email is human-toned (WF-08 sequence B).
  • Stripe payout schedule — confirmed; bank account verified.
  • Operator can pause ads — admin access to all ad accounts confirmed; pause button known.
  • Backup operator — at least one other person has access to GHL + Stripe + ad accounts in case of emergency.
Gate to traffic If any of the above fails, do not turn on paid spend. Fix first, retest the failed row, then proceed to the launch-day runbook.
