Launch Blueprint · Day 1

Walk it minute by minute on launch day.

A time-boxed runbook for the day you flip ads on. Don't improvise. The job today is to validate that real spend produces real purchase_ebook7 events without surprises — not to scale.

Prereq. The preflight QA is fully green and the pre-launch checks are walked. If either is incomplete, do not start this runbook.

T-24h · The day before

Final calm sweep

  1. Re-run the test purchase — confirm pixel + WF-02 still fire.
  2. Send yourself a fresh test freebie opt-in; confirm WF-01 day-0 email arrives.
  3. Refresh the GHL Reporting → Dashboard so the numbers start from zero in your head.
  4. Stage all creatives in the ad accounts as paused. Do not enable yet.
  5. Triple-check the destination URL and UTMs on every paused ad — paste them into a spreadsheet and visually diff.
  6. Sleep. The launch isn't about the first hour, it's about the first 7 days.

T-0 · Morning of

Open the doors

  1. Open: GHL conversations, GHL contacts smart list (last 24h), Stripe dashboard, ad-platform manager, Meta Pixel Helper, the live sales page in incognito.
  2. Run one final real $7 test purchase from a clean device. Watch the sequence: pixel → thank-you page → GHL tag → WF-02 delivery email.
  3. If anything misses, stop and fix. If everything fires, proceed.
  4. Enable cold-ebook7 ad sets one at a time, broad first, lookalikes second.
  5. Set a weekly budget ceiling in your head. Don't think in daily numbers.
  6. Post the day-1 organic Reel from the content calendar.

T+1h to T+4h

Watch, do not adjust

  1. Resist the urge to optimize anything in the first 4 hours. Platforms need time to learn.
  2. Watch refund / support inbox. If the same question comes from 3 separate buyers, fix the asset that caused it before the next batch.
  3. Confirm: at least one impression on every active ad, at least one click on the highest-spend ad set, no policy / rejected notifications.

T+4h to T+24h

Validate the path

  1. Look for the first real-world purchase_ebook7. If it landed, the wiring is real.
  2. Spot-check a buyer contact — confirm buyer-ebook7 applied, WF-02 step 1 sent, UTMs captured.
  3. Reply to the first 3 inbound emails personally. Set the tone.
  4. Do not change budget, audience, or creative inside 24h.

Day 2

Sanity check, then leave it alone

  1. Pull last 24h: spend, link clicks, $7 purchases, refunds, opt-outs.
  2. If CPP is clearly above target with at least one ad set having spent >1.5× target — keep ad set, kill its losing creative variant.
  3. If zero conversions across the whole campaign at total spend = 3× target CPP — pause cold-ebook7, replace creatives. Audience first move = wrong move.
  4. Post day-2 organic Reel. Reply to comments inside 4 hours.

Days 3–7

First scale window

  1. Step working ad sets up by 20% per 24h, no more.
  2. Add the cart-retargeting ad set on day 3 if abandoned-checkout audience has at least 100 matched users.
  3. Refresh fatigued creatives mid-week with the next batch from the swipe file.
  4. Read 30-day LTV at day 30, not before. Do not panic at day-3 ROAS.

Emergency stop conditions

  • Buyer contact created without WF-02 firing → pause spend, fix the trigger.
  • Refund rate > 10% inside 48 hours → pause spend, audit the promise on the sales page.
  • Pixel stops firing across the site → pause spend, fix tag, then resume.
  • Stripe / payment provider flags account → pause spend until cleared.
  • Operator can't reach support inbox for >24h → pause spend until coverage is restored.
