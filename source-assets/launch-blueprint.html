What the blueprint does not do

It does not connect your accounts. The dashboard reports placeholder vs. ready, the validator warns you when production fields are unset, but only you can paste the real GHL location ID, real Stripe-connected checkout URLs, real Skool URLs, real Meta Pixel ID, and real Marketplace OAuth client ID into config.js and the Vercel environment.

It does not promise costs or conversion rates. The math in the calculator is for modeling, not forecasting. Real numbers come from real spend and real creative.
