Launch Blueprint · Email & SMS

Ready-to-paste sequences for every workflow.

Drop these messages directly into GoHighLevel. Each block names the workflow, the step delay, and the merge fields. SMS includes the legally required STOP clause on the first message of each sequence. Personalize names and product titles, but keep the structure.

Compliance. GHL respects unsubscribe and STOP automatically when the contact carries unsubscribed-email or sms-opt-out tags (configured in the GHL Build Sheet). Every email must include the physical address and unsubscribe link in the footer; first SMS in any sequence must include the opt-out instruction.
